What You Get

  • ✔ Stadium information for your booking
  • ✔ Kickoff time confirmations
  • ✔ Access routes and recommended gates
  • ✔ Bag policy and entry requirements
  • ✔ Quick replies on WhatsApp (BAG / ROUTE)

How It Works

MatchDay Navigator sends match‑day details to WhatsApp using approved templates. You’ll receive stadium info, kickoff time, access route, and bag rules for your booking.

  • Open the message we send on WhatsApp
  • Tap the Open route button to see your access route
  • Reply BAG or ROUTE to get more details

Trusted Delivery via WhatsApp

MatchDay Navigator uses the WhatsApp Business API (via Bird) to securely deliver booking‑specific information. Your data is used only to provide this service.
